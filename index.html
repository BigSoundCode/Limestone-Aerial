About Us

Our Story

Limestone Aerial is a Kingston based Unmanned Aviation Vehicle (UAV) service provider committed to helping organizations become more efficient and effective using drone technology. Businesses are experiencing a need to adapt new approaches and Limestone Aerial has the capacity to enhance modern business practices by integrating drone-based technology into their services. Limestone Aerial can assist with applications in industries ranging from construction and surveying to real estate and general aerial photography support.

About the Founder

Limestone Aerial was founded by Kyle Smallegange, a professional engineer with a background in the construction industry. Kyle is passionate to integrate state of the art approaches and apply technology to solve challenges experienced by individuals or organizations. Kyle merged this passion and his technical ability within the construction and engineering industries and established Limestone Aerial.

Why Us?

Limestone Aerial is dedicated to providing exceptional services to our clients and helping them excel within their industry. We promise that every project is executed safely and efficiently and ensure that our clients receive the products that they require.

Services

Promotional Aerial Footage

We can create promotional content for your next project or event. We capture aerial images including photographs and videos and provide editing services. Whether it is for a wedding, public event, or a record of your property, we are here to tailor our services to fit your needs!


Real Estate

Whether you are a homeowner, real estate agent, or photographer, we can provide aerial images/videos to help showcase your property and enhance your listings. We can provide products ranging from basic data to full edited video that suits your needs.


Photogrammetry and Aerial Mapping Services

We provide photogrammetry services, which is a method used to obtain accurate and reliable data for mapping purposes, combining multiple images captured by a drone and creating a measurable 3D model. This can be an extremely useful tool in the architecture and design industry. Our services include measurable cloud point data that can be exported in various formats. For more information on these services, please give us a call!


Construction Progress Reports

Pictures speak a thousand words. Give your construction team full access to visual documentation through various stages of the project using a cloud-based software. The interactive software allows team members to collaborate from remote locations, improving communication, reducing costs and saving time by using aerial data to perform inspection and document progress.


Infrastructure Inspections

In the past, infrastructure which has been challenging to access has been inspected using conventional methods, for example, from aerial lifts, swing stages, and even helicopters. These methods can be very costly, time consuming, and can be unsafe. The use of drone technology can dramatically reduce the cost and time required to complete these inspections, is much safer than conventional approaches, and with the appropriate equipment, can collect the most relevant data.


Roof Inspection and Take-Off Reports

Critical records such as pre-construction footage for design purposes, warranty images throughout construction or completion of a roofing project, we are here to help. Our equipment is capable of measuring roofs and creating a comprehensive roof report, which provides a property overview, surface overview, perimeter, pitch and surface area quantities, edge measurements, and edge type.


Surveying and Data Collection

Looking for a topographic survey of your property? Our team can conduct topographic surveys within a matter of minutes and provide deliverables that can include cut and fill reports, DXF files, 3D models using photogrammetry, and much more. Feel free to give us a call for more information.
